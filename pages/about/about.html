About Me

Who Am I?

I am an experience lawyer with a law degree from Columbia University. After graduating summa cum laude, I interned at Landman and Zach before turning down a permanent position to start a firm. With my college friend Foggy Nelson, I started the firm Nelson and Murdock. After shutting down due to complications, Nelson and Murdock returned with the new firm Nelson, Murdock, and Page, where I continue to work today.
